Team DevOps

Posted on August 4, 2020 at 12:00 PM

Mission

Our mission is to ensure that our services are provided to cities across the world by configuring processes that automate operational tasks.

Project Description

DevOps aims to support all three development teams as they build their unique applications. Utilizing Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD) automated processes, the applications are well-maintained and provisioned with automated communication tools that keep the development teams informed with detailed reports. Their codes will be integrated through Github repositories for continuous integration, tested with Sonarqube, and automatically deployed to Heroku for continuous delivery. Heroku is utilized as the platform to build, deliver, and monitor all the applciations being built in a manner that meets or exceeds financial expectations.
