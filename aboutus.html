About Us

We are a team of professionals and students from varying backgrounds who have come together in this platform to create a service that many municipalities in the world would benefit from. The aim is to guide users to various parts of their municipalities with much needed information.

We present users with information to their queries. Queries may range from "can I plant trees in this area?" to "nearest construction project." The chatbot provides very useful information based on search-type and is custom to every question. Just ask..
