Mission

Project Description

The Municipality Permit Chatbot System (MPCS) is to design, develop, and deliver a reliable and functional software application that assists end-users in navigating the local city website (e.g., Pasadena) by answering questions and providing information when inquired. Questions provided by the end-user are administered via a popup website chatbox. The MPCS convincingly acknowledges and interfaces with the end-user comparable to a human customer representative by determining whether to provide a direct answer or reference search results from within the website.

Furthermore, the MPCS is linked to other applications within the website to provide accurate information to the end-user. The MPCS will also contain options that will be customizable by its stakeholders (e.g., City Officials) through a downloadable client. Each stage of the project will require approval from key stakeholders to transition to the subsequent phase to ensure the project has adequate coverage in meeting the stakeholder's performance expectations.
