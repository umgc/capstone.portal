Mentoring At UMGC

The UMCG Capstone Project benefits from the much needed individualized assistance offered by mentors to help students and the professor alike to achieve excellence during the course of the project. They are passionate and have an extensive knowledge in the field of Information Technology. Such wealth of knowledge in the industry grants students a wider avenue of creativity, development, guidance, and positive long-term relationships.
